Description

What is eExamination?

eExamination is a complete system where you can create and conduct online examination for your institutes, organizations, coaching centers, etc.
Who can use eExamination?

eExamination online examination portal is best for the institutes or coaching centers providing classes for the competitive examinations like Physics, Maths etc. This portal is specially developed for the small and medium scale institutes. But the world class design and user friendly environment makes eExamination a choice for any reputed Institution.
What are the features of this Online Examination Portal ?

i.You can create unlimited number of student profiles.
ii.The system will create user id and passwords for every student automatically and send it to their email id.
iii.You can insert and modify unlimited questions sets.
iv.You can create and conduct unlimited examinations.
v.You can publish and view results of student.
vi.You can notify every student about the examinations or share study materials through this system.
vii.You can also create different users on administrative label and provide them separate permissions for different work (for example, a data entry operator can upload student details but cannot create examinations).
Why use eExamination?

i.It will reduce the cost and time of organizing examinations.
ii.Students can evaluate themselves by giving test exams as many times as they need.
iii.It will check answers and publish results in few seconds.
iv.Students can appear in the examination through any devices (i.e. Laptops, Android devices, Windows devices, iphones, any smart phone, etc.)
v.It is in online platform so, no software installation required.
vi.It will reduce the use of paper which in turn saves trees.
